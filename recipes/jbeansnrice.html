Jamaican Beans and Rice

Description

Sally Fallon's timeless Jamaican Beans and Rice recipe, as featured in her classic cookbook, Nourishing Traditions.

Ingredients

  • Kidney beans (1 cup)
  • Brown rice (1 cup)
  • Coconut milk (1 can)
  • Jalapenos (6)
  • Green onions (1 bunch)
  • Thyme (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt (1 tablespoon)

Steps

  1. Soak beans for 24 hours
  2. Drain beans and place in pressure cooker with chopped jalapenos, greeon onions, thyme, salt, and coconut milk
  3. Pressure cook on high for 45 minutes
  4. While beans are cooking, bring water to a boil
  5. Add brown rice to boiling water
  6. Cook brown rice for 45 minutes
  7. When both rice and beans are done cooking, mix 'em together!
