Munchies Halal Food

Description

Munchies Halal food is a premier destination at which to buy your dinner nightly. You must eat there every night to get the full effect.

Ingredients

  • Lamb (many chunks)
  • Rice (spiced up)
  • Fries
  • Salad (chopped up iceberg, tomatoes, onions, and cucumbers)
  • White sauce
  • Hot sauce
  • Green sauce

Steps

  1. Get out of work, probably after midnight
  2. Be really hungry
  3. Take the subway home
  4. Go to Munchies - it's the only food place that's open, unless you want a deli sandwich
  5. When you walk in, they already know what you want because you go there every night - lamb over rice, fries, salad, white, hot, and green
  6. Pay them the cost of the food plus a 20% tip
  7. Eat at home in front of TV with extra sauce (Valentina) to taste
