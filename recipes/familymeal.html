Family Meal

Description

A great way to eat on a budget. It's not free, but it's cheap and usually delicious!

Ingredients

  • Restaurant
  • Kitchen staff
  • FOH staff
  • Assorted managers and chefs
  • Big pot of food
  • Plates
  • Tabasco or cholula

Steps

  1. Get to work at 3:00
  2. Family meal starts at 4:00
  3. Eat the food - be sure to wear your bib
  4. Finish up with an espresso and a cigarette
